WELCOME TO KGMS

Krishnaswamy Nagar Ganga Nagar Matriculation School is one that has been recognised by the Department of Education, Tamil Nadu. The medium of instruction is English and it is preparing the students for the Samacheer Examination conducted by the Government of Tamil Nadu. It is run by the Amenities and Welfare Association of the Krishnaswamy Nagar Ganga Nagar of which at present the President is Mr. R. Kalyanasundaram. The Founder Secretary was M.A.L.T., Former Headmaster Sri. N. Chinnaswamy Naidu and Correspondent of Mani Higher Secondary School, Pappanickenpalayam, Coimbatore. After the demise of Sri. N. Chinnaswamy Naidu, Sri. Rangaswamy, Sri. E. Kasthuri, Sri. P.V. Rajkumar, Prof. R.V. Guruswamy & Sri. K. Damodaran were Secretary and Correspondent. The Past Correspondents took keen interest for the betterment of the school which is reflected in obtaining 100% Results in Public Examination for the last twenty five years consecutively. Our present Secretary and Correspondent is Sri. B. Srinivasan. Sri. N. Chinnaswamy Naidu M.A.L.T., a President of Krishnaswamy Nagar realised the need of good education to the Lower Middle Class and Salaried Class and started in a humble way a Children's School from L.K.G. to V Std 1971. It was opened by the Eminent Educationist of our State and former Director of School Education, Tamilnadu and Vice-Chancellor of Madras University, Sri. N.D. Sundaravadivelu under the esteemed presidentship of eminent Educationist of our District and former Principal of PSG College of Technology, Coimbatore Sri. G.R. Damodran M.P. on 14-4-71. After successful running for about a decade, the school was converted into a Matriculation School; and got recognised by the Department of Education of our State.
