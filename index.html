Welcome to KRISHNASWAMY NAGAR GANGA NAGAR MATRICULATION HIGHER SECONDARY SCHOOL. Our mission is to provide quality education to our students.

SCHOOL MOTTO "SMILE AND SERVE" is our School Motto It is to; be brave and courageous always. Be ready to face every odd experience in life with smile on your face. Be balanced in life under all circumstances joy or sorrow, success or failure, honour or dishonour and prosperity or adversity. This calmness of the mind expresses itself in an eternal smile. It makes you ready to be of service to one and all without caste, creed or religion. It makes you perfect.
